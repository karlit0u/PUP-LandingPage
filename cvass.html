CVASS

Guidelines

1.0 GET TICKET CODE

Contact the specific office/transaction/services at cvass-contacts/services

2.0 OPEN CVASS LINK

Open the link sent to your email or visit bit.ly/cvass2021v2

2.1 ENTER APPOINTMENT INFORMATION

Create/Set Appointment with a valid ticket code

2.2 SUBMIT APPOINTMENT

Submit your appointment request and wait for the confirmation thru email

3.0 RECEIVE NOTIFICATION /CONFIRMATION

Status, updates and notification will be sent to your registered email address. You may also monitor the status of your request at bit.ly/3nEJCXk

4.0 VERIFY APPOINTMENT STATUS

If your request has been confirmed, the status of your appointment will be marked as approved. You can check the status at bit.ly/3nEJCXk

5.0 RECEIVE GATE PASS

A Gate Pass will be sent to your registered email a day or two before your appointment date.

6.0 VISIT CAMPUS

Show the Email Confirmation /Gate Pass and a valid ID to the PUPSRC Security Personnel. You are not allowed to enter the Campus premises if you do not have an Email Confirmation /Gate Pass
